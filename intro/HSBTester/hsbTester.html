HSB Tester: Guess the color

A more natural way for us (humans) to think about color is Hue-Saturation-Brightness (HSB). Given a random color code, try to guess the color that it represents. First pick a color resolution (bpc) and generate a color. Then choose a hue from the color wheel (think about what are the dominant colors in the code). Then you'll be able to see the different saturations for that hue (remember what grey is?) and finally choose a brightness.
