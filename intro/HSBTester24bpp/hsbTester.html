HSB Tester: Guess the color

A more natural way for us (humans) to think about color is Hue-Saturation-Brightness (HSB). Given random color values, try to guess the actual color that it represents. First generate a color. Then choose a hue by clicking on the color wheel: think about what are the dominant color(s) based on the values. Then you'll be able to see the different saturations for that hue (remember what grey is?) and finally choose a brightness.
